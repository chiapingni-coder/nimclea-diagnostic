## Remaining WARNING Items

| Area | Warning |
| --- | --- |
| Alias rank ambiguity | Generic aliases such as `completed`, `ready`, and `active` can over-rank ambiguous backend values. |
| Backend ownership proof | Helper rejects known fallback sources, including compound source strings, but cannot prove a record is backend-owned when source metadata is absent. |

## Top 3 Risks

| Rank | Risk | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Source-less local records can still be evaluated by backend-owned helper functions if callers pass them in. | The helper blocks marked fallback data, but cannot infer ownership without metadata. |
